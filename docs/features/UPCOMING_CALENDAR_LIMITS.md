# Upcoming Calendar Limits

This manual explains the feature that limits the number of upcoming calendar entries per member.

## 1. What Schedule Limits Do

Schedule limits prevent excessive future bookings by capping how many upcoming events a member can hold.

## 2. Organization Settings

Admins/owners can configure:
1. `Enable Schedule Limit`
2. `Maximum future events`
3. `Enforce limit for instructors and DPEs`

## 3. Member Experience

When enabled:
1. You can book normally until you reach your limit.
2. Once at the limit, new bookings are blocked until you reduce upcoming events.
3. If your organization has checked the `Enforce limit for instructors and DPEs` checkbox, then it may be difficult for instructors or DPEs to schedule multiple aircraft checkouts. Consider using the `Enable reserve on behalf` option.

## 4. How to Resolve Limit Errors

1. Cancel or reschedule unneeded upcoming events.
2. Wait until upcoming events are completed.
3. If needed, ask admin/owner to review organization limit settings.

## 5. Best Practices

1. Keep only realistic upcoming bookings.
2. Release slots early when plans change.
3. Avoid placeholder bookings that block others.
