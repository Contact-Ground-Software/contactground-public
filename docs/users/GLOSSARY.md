# Glossary

Standard terms used across Contact Ground documentation.

## Core Terms

1. `Contact Ground`: the application.
2. `Organization`: your tenant/team inside Contact Ground.
3. `Member`: a user with membership in an organization.
4. `Role`: permissions assigned to a member (`owner`, `admin`, `operations`, `accounting`, etc.).
5. `Resource`: a schedulable asset (aircraft, simulator, other equipment).

## Scheduling And Operations

1. `Flight Operations` (`Flight Ops`): workflows for post-flight data, status tracking, and operational oversight.
2. `Event`: a calendar entry (flight, training, maintenance, availability, etc.).
3. `Schedule Limit`: max future bookings allowed per member (org setting).
4. `Availability Request`: a request sent to instructors/evaluators for schedule availability.
5. `Dispatch Board`: older shorthand for the `Flight Operations` page and its operations-oriented activity view.

## Maintenance And Safety

1. `Squawk`: a reported issue/discrepancy on a resource.
2. `Maintenance`: workflow for tracking and resolving squawks and maintenance items.
3. `Checklist`: pre/post-flight checklist assigned to resources.

## Billing And Accounting

1. `Invoice`: billing document generated for members.
2. `Credit`: amount that reduces future invoice totals.
3. `Credit Request`: member-submitted reimbursement request that can become a credit if approved.
4. `Invoicing Mode`:
   - `Manual Invoicing`: billing and payment status handled in Contact Ground.
   - `QuickBooks Integration`: invoice/credit sync with QuickBooks Online.
5. `QuickBooks Realm ID`: company identifier for the connected QuickBooks Online account.

## Permissions And Qualification

1. `Endorsement`: qualification used to control resource access or special capabilities.
2. `Blog Endorsement`: endorsement that can allow members to post/edit News entries.
3. `Leadership Roles`: owner/admin-level roles with broader operational control.

## Messaging And Communication

1. `Organizational Messaging`: sending announcements to selected groups (individuals, roles, endorsements, whole org).
2. `Target Mode`: recipient selection mode in messaging.
3. `Reply Thread`: responses to an organization message shown in message review views.

## Terminology Standards

Use these preferred terms in docs:

1. Prefer `Flight Operations` over `Flight Ops` in headings and first reference.
2. Prefer `Squawk` for maintenance issue records.
3. Prefer `Resource` unless the context is explicitly aircraft-only.
