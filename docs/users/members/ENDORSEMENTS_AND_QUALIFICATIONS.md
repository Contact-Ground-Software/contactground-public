# Endorsements and Qualifications

This guide explains member-facing endorsement behavior in Contact Ground.

## 1. What Endorsements Are

Endorsements are qualification records assigned by your organization.

They are used to:
1. Control access to endorsement-restricted resources.
2. Grant selected feature access (for example, `Blog` posting in `News`).

## 2. Where to View Your Endorsements

1. Open `People`.
2. Find your own member record/card.
3. Review endorsement badges.

## 3. How Endorsements Affect Booking

When a resource requires endorsements, booking checks enforce those requirements.

If you are missing required endorsements, the booking flow may not allow the reservation.

## 4. Getting a New Endorsement

Members cannot self-assign endorsements.

To add one:
1. Complete the required training/checkout process.
2. Ask an authorized instructor/admin to update your record.

## 5. Endorsement Removal

If an endorsement is removed:
1. Booking access tied to that endorsement is removed.
2. Existing/future reservations may need operations review depending on policy.

## 6. Special Case: Blog Endorsement

`Blog` endorsement can allow a standard member to post/edit their own `News` entries.
