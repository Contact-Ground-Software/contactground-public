# Member Notification Preferences

This manual explains how members control notification delivery and notification types.

## 1. Where To Manage Preferences

Open `Profile` from the sidebar. You will see two notification areas:
1. `Notification Methods` (how notifications are delivered)
2. `Notification Preferences` (which categories you receive for your active organization)

## 2. Notification Methods (Email, SMS, Push)

In `Notification Methods`, you can enable or disable:
1. Email notifications
2. SMS notifications
3. Push notifications

Important notes:
1. SMS requires a verified phone number and explicit SMS consent.
2. If SMS is disabled, verify your phone first, then enable `SMS Notifications` and accept the consent dialog.
3. Changes in this section are saved with the profile `Save` button.
4. SMS disclosure is available at `/policies/sms-consent.html`.
5. By opting into SMS, you also consent to receive core platform notification types such as invoicing, organizational messaging, and flight operations alerts.

## 3. Organization Notification Categories

In the `Notification Preferences` section, you can turn categories on or off for the active organization:
1. Schedule updates
2. Weather changes
3. Instructor availability
4. Availability requests
5. News updates
6. Currency reminders
7. Preflight dispatch timing

Category toggles save immediately when changed.

## 4. What Each Category Means

1. `Schedule Updates`:
Created, changed, or canceled events affecting you.
2. `Weather Changes`:
Significant weather changes relevant to upcoming flights.
3. `Instructor Availability`:
Instructor availability posted for training scheduling.
4. `Availability Requests`:
Requests sent to you for availability (primarily instructor/DPE workflows).
5. `News Updates`:
Notifications when your organization publishes a new News entry. Email, push, and in-app links open the exact entry when supported.
6. `Currency Reminders`:
Notices for organization-defined and personal reminder items approaching expiration.
7. `Preflight Dispatch Timing`:
Choose how far before a scheduled flight Contact Ground should send your dispatch brief. Supported choices are `At event time`, `15 minutes`, `30 minutes`, `45 minutes`, or `1 hour before`.

Important:
1. The default is `30 minutes before`.
2. This timing applies only when your organization uses `Automatic` flight operations mode.
3. In `Manual` mode, operations follow-up is manual and preflight dispatch briefs are not sent automatically.

## 5. Recommended Setup

1. Keep `Schedule Updates` enabled.
2. Keep at least one delivery method enabled (email or push recommended).
3. Keep `News Updates` enabled if you want to hear about new organization announcements without checking `News` manually.
4. Enable SMS only after phone verification.
5. Re-check preferences whenever you change roles or organizations.
6. If you rely on preflight dispatch reminders, choose a reminder time that matches your normal prep window.

## 6. Troubleshooting

1. Not receiving anything:
Confirm at least one delivery method is enabled in profile settings.
2. Not receiving only one type:
Check that category toggle in `Notification Preferences` is enabled.
3. SMS not available:
Verify your phone number in profile first.
4. Not receiving a preflight dispatch brief:
Confirm your organization is in `Automatic` flight operations mode and that your selected reminder time has not already passed long before the event was created.
